VACANT LOT COMMUNITY GARDEN USE AGREEMENT

This Vacant Lot Community Garden Use Agreement (“Use Agreement”) is entered into on January 20, 2026, by and between Mason Corner Properties, LLC (“Owner”) and Oakview Garden Circle (“Garden Group”).

1. Premises
Owner permits Garden Group to use the vacant lot located at 611 Mason Avenue, DeKalb County, Georgia (the “Lot”) for seasonal community gardening activities.

2. Permitted Use
Garden Group may use the Lot solely for noncommercial community gardening, including planting vegetables, flowers, pollinator habitat, composting in enclosed bins, volunteer workdays, and related educational activities. The Lot shall not be used for retail sales, overnight storage of heavy equipment, or permanent structures without Owner’s prior written consent.

3. Term
The term of this Use Agreement begins on February 1, 2026, and expires on November 30, 2026, unless renewed in writing by the parties.

4. Renewal
Any renewal must be in writing and signed by both parties no later than fifteen (15) days before the expiration of the current term. Absent a signed renewal, Garden Group shall remove movable tools, bins, and supplies from the Lot by the expiration date.

5. Maintenance Obligations
Garden Group shall be responsible for routine upkeep of the Lot, including trash removal, bed maintenance, mowing or trimming of overgrown common areas, and securing tools after volunteer activities. Owner shall remain responsible for structural conditions outside Garden Group’s reasonable control, including latent underground utility issues and preexisting perimeter wall defects.

6. Liability and Risk
Garden Group accepts the Lot in its current condition and shall use reasonable care during activities. Garden Group shall promptly report known hazardous conditions to Owner.

7. Access
Garden Group may access the Lot between sunrise and sunset unless otherwise agreed in writing.

8. Termination
Owner may terminate this Use Agreement upon ten (10) days’ written notice for material noncompliance, including repeated unsafe storage, prohibited use, or failure to maintain the Lot in reasonably orderly condition. Either party may terminate for convenience upon twenty-one (21) days’ written notice.

9. Governing Law
This Use Agreement shall be governed by Georgia law.
