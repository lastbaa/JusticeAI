AFFIDAVIT OF SARAH KIM

State of Georgia
County of DeKalb

Before me, the undersigned officer, personally appeared Sarah Kim, who being duly sworn states as follows:

1. My name is Sarah Kim. I am over eighteen years of age and competent to make this affidavit.

2. I have volunteered at the Oakview community garden in DeKalb County on several weekend workdays during the spring of 2026.

3. On Saturday, April 6, 2026, I arrived at the garden shortly before 9:00 a.m. Present at that time were Jerome Lee, Anita Brooks, and two newer volunteers whose first names I understood to be Marcus and Elena.

4. I observed Jerome unlock the storage shed. The shed was not left open for general access. People took tools as directed and returned them after use.

5. I also observed a small plastic container of gardening chemicals or treatment products stored on an upper shelf inside the shed. I did not handle the container, but I remember it because Anita told one of the newer volunteers not to move it.

6. Later that morning, I heard Anita say that only approved volunteers were supposed to handle stored treatment products and fertilizer.

7. During the same workday, I saw bags of mulch and ordinary hand tools near the side gate for part of the morning. By the time I left around noon, the tools had been put away.

8. I did not observe anyone pouring chemicals, spraying plants, or storing loose materials outside the marked storage areas that day.

9. I make this affidavit based on my personal observations.

Further affiant sayeth not.

Sworn to and subscribed before me this 14th day of May, 2026.
