COMMUNITY GARDEN MAINTENANCE SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Community Garden Maintenance Services Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into on March 1, 2026, by and between Eastside Growers Collective, Inc. (“Client”), a Georgia nonprofit organization operating neighborhood garden plots in DeKalb County, Georgia, and Red Clay Garden Services LLC (“Contractor”).

1. Purpose
Client desires to retain Contractor to provide routine maintenance services for the community garden located at 1843 Glenwood Road, DeKalb County, Georgia (the “Site”). Contractor agrees to provide such services under the terms set forth below.

2. Term
The initial term of this Agreement shall begin on March 15, 2026, and continue through October 31, 2026, unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 8.

3. Scope of Services
Contractor shall provide the following routine services at the Site:
(a) weeding planting beds and common walking paths;
(b) monitoring irrigation lines for visible leaks or blockages;
(c) removing dead plant material and seasonal debris;
(d) trimming overgrowth along the perimeter fence and storage shed area;
(e) reporting observed safety hazards to Client’s program coordinator; and
(f) performing one monthly seasonal cleanup that includes bagging yard waste and consolidating discarded materials for pickup.

Contractor shall perform services in a manner consistent with ordinary horticultural practice for small urban garden sites in DeKalb County. Contractor shall not apply pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers unless specifically authorized in writing by Client.

4. Service Schedule
Routine maintenance shall be performed once each week during the term. Seasonal cleanup shall be performed once per calendar month. Except in the event of severe weather or unsafe site conditions, weekly service shall occur no later than five business days after the beginning of each service week.

5. Compensation
Client shall pay Contractor a flat service fee of $600 per month for routine maintenance and seasonal cleanup. Contractor shall submit one invoice at the end of each calendar month. Payment shall be due within fifteen (15) days after Client’s receipt of a proper invoice. Any additional work requested by Client outside the Scope of Services shall be billed only if approved in writing in advance at a rate of $45 per hour.

6. Access and Coordination
Client shall provide Contractor with reasonable access to the Site between 8:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m., Monday through Saturday. Contractor shall coordinate major cleanup activity with Client’s program coordinator when volunteers are scheduled to be on-site.

7. Standard of Performance
Contractor shall use commercially reasonable care in performing the services. Contractor shall promptly notify Client of any visible irrigation failure, fence breach, or unsafe accumulation of tools or materials.

8. Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon fourteen (14) days’ written notice to the other party. Client may terminate this Agreement immediately for material breach, including repeated failure to perform scheduled maintenance, unauthorized chemical application, or unsafe conduct at the Site. Contractor may terminate immediately if Client fails to provide Site access for more than ten (10) consecutive days or fails to pay undisputed invoices within thirty (30) days after written notice of nonpayment.

9. Independent Contractor Status
Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee, partner, or agent of Client.

10. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Georgia.

11. Notices
Any notice under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by hand, email, or certified mail to the addresses designated by the parties. Notice shall be effective on the date of delivery if delivered by hand or email, or three business days after mailing if sent by certified mail.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
